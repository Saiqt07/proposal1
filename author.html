About the Team

We are a team of second-year college students from the University of the East, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. As passionate learners and digital natives, we recognize both the power and pitfalls of technology in our daily lives — particularly for students like us.

Our Mission

Our group created the Digital Wellness as a project dedicated to promoting healthy tech habits. This platform is a resource space for students and professionals alike, covering crucial topics like blue light exposure, social media detox, productivity apps, ergonomics, and mindful tech use. We believe that by learning to manage screen time and digital distractions, individuals can lead more focused, balanced, and fulfilling lives in today's fast-paced digital environment.

Through this initiative, we aim not just to educate others, but also to grow ourselves — blending our tech skills with human-centered thinking. Every guide, tool, and topic shared on this site reflects our research, collaboration, and shared passion for digital wellbeing and productivity.
